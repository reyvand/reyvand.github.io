About Us

Macskot hostel delivers the perfect combination of surf, beaches, culture and relaxation in the 'HEART' of uluwatu. Our location right in the central of all favourite beaches in south of bali (with 10-20 minutes driving) to the famous Green bowl beach, Padang-Padang beach, Bingin beach, uluwatu beach, Dreamland beach, Balangan beach, Jimbaran beach, nusa dua beach, atm center ,local warung ,minimarket and only take 20 minutes (5 km) from Ngurah Rai Airport. if you cannot drive scooter its not a big problem because we have a shuttle 3 times a day to the beaches and any attraction in uluwatu with the low and fair price.

All day long we provide , coffee, tea, mineral water and all are free of charge. Within walking around 10 minutes, guests easily can find many local foods starting from 1 USD , restaurant, convenience store, ATM.

We also offer a daily shuttle everyday and airport transfer, surf class, surf board rental, motorbike rental, towel rental, laundry service, package for going to Gili Trawangan Island/ Gili Air Island/ Nusa Lembongan Island/ Lombok Island all include with the shuttle pick up at the Hostel.

Want to explore a mountain? We also have an offer for hiking to Mt. Batur, Mt. Rinjani, Mt. Agung, and etc.
